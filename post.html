Today has been...interesting for you.

It started normally, what with going to school, volunteering to donate blood for some organization you forgot the name of the moment you finished, heading home to do homework as usual...

Then the next thing you know you hear pounding on your door, and a minute later you're talking to some guy in a uniform you don't recognize who's panting and sweating - apparently having literally chased you back home - and babbling incoherently, You didn't catch much - something about "100% compatibility" and "one of a kind for our work," though once he caught his breath he told you that he wanted to recruit you for something called the "Chaldea Security Organization" - he didn't say what the work was but he handed over documents that seemed legitimate enough.
